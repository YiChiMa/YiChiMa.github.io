Research

My research focuses on algorithms that enable autonomous agents to learn complex behaviors efficiently and safely. I'm currently working on several projects centered around decarbonization technologies. My ultimate goal is to create reliable autonomous agents that can assist us with real-world decision making and control as we address the challenge of climate change.
